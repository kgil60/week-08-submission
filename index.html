Motionless In White

History, Members, and Discography

Motionless in White is a metalcore band from Scranton Pennsylvania. Formed in 2004, they have released 6 full length albums

Current Members:

  • Chris "Motionless" Cerulli (vocals)
  • Ryan Sitkowski (guitar)
  • Ricky "Horror" Olsen (guitar)
  • Vinny Mauro (drums)
  • Justin Morrow (bass)
